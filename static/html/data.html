ANALYSIS OF MODEL DATA

Overview

Data for this initial analysis was sourced from Kaggle, utilizing chest X-rays compiled by the National Institute of Health (NIH). The dataset includes 112,120 X-ray images from 30,805 unique patients. Patient information has been analyzed based on gender, view position, and age.

Data Source: Wang X, Peng Y, Lu L, Lu Z, Bagheri M, Summers RM. ChestX-ray8: Hospital-scale Chest X-ray Database and Benchmarks on Weakly-Supervised Classification and Localization of Common Thorax Diseases. IEEE CVPR 2017, ChestX-ray8Hospital-ScaleChestCVPR2017_paper.pdf
