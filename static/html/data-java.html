ANALYSIS OF MODEL DATA - INTERACTIVE

Background

Access all diagnoses associated with a CXR, as well as the identified triage level and highest-level diagnosis.

Four different triage levels have been delineated:

No Finding: The CXR has no identifiable diagnoses. Green zone.

Chronic: The identified diagnosis is not of immediate concern, but the patient should still seek treatment with a long-term care provider. Yellow zone.
    Example: Cardiomegaly (AKA an enlarged heart)

Acute: The identified diagnosis is of immediate concern and requires within a twenty-four-hour window, but is not life-threatening. Orange zone.
    Example: Pneumonia

Emergent: The identified diagnosis is of immediate concern and requires treatment as soon as possible, as the condition is life-threatening or requires surgical intervention. Red zone.
    Example: Pneumothorax (AKA a collapsed lung)
